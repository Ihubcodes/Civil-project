1. Excavation

  • Process: Digging the foundation area according to the house plan.
  • Materials/Equipment:
    • Excavator or backhoe for digging.
    • Shovels and spades for manual work.
    • Dump trucks for soil removal.

2. Foundation Work

  • Process: Laying a solid base for the structure.
  • Materials:
    • Cement
    • Sand
    • Gravel or crushed stone
    • Steel reinforcement bars (TMT bars)
    • Waterproofing materials
    • Formwork (wood or metal for shaping concrete)

3. Layout and Earth Work

  • Process: Marking boundaries and preparing the area for foundation.
  • Materials:
    • Surveying equipment (tape measures, markers)
    • Bracing materials for deep excavations (wood or metal)

4. Anti-Termite Treatment

  • Process: Protecting the structure from termite damage.
  • Materials:
    • Termiticides (chemical treatment)
    • Barrier systems (physical or chemical)

5. Formwork (Shuttering)

  • Process: Temporary molds to hold concrete until it sets.
  • Materials:
    • Plywood or metal sheets
    • Supporting beams and clamps
    • Releasing agents

6. Concrete Work

  • Process: Mixing and pouring concrete for the foundation and structures.
  • Materials:
    • Cement
    • Sand
    • Aggregate (gravel or crushed stone)
    • Water
    • Concrete mixer

7. Masonry Work

  • Process: Building walls with bricks or blocks.
  • Materials:
    • Bricks or concrete blocks
    • Mortar (sand, cement, and water mix)
    • Trowels for spreading mortar

8. Reinforced Cement Concrete (RCC) Work

  • Process: Strengthening concrete with steel bars.
  • Materials:
    • TMT bars
    • Concrete mix
    • Vibrators for compacting concrete

9. Curing

  • Process: Keeping concrete moist to gain strength.
  • Materials:
    • Water (for soaking)
    • Jute bags or other covering materials

10. Plastering

  • Process: Covering walls to smoothen surfaces.
  • Materials:
    • Cement
    • Sand
    • Water

11. Waterproofing

  • Process: Preventing water leakage in walls and roofs.
  • Materials:
    • Waterproofing compounds
    • Damp-proof membranes

12. Flooring

  • Process: Installing the flooring surface.
  • Materials:
    • Tiles or flooring boards
    • Adhesives or grout
    • Underlayment

13. Electrical Work

  • Process: Installing electrical systems.
  • Materials:
    • Wires and cables
    • Switches and sockets
    • Circuit breakers
    • Electrical panels

14. Plumbing and Sanitation

  • Process: Installing water supply and drainage systems.
  • Materials:
    • Pipes (PVC, CPVC, or copper)
    • Fittings (elbows, tees, couplings)
    • Fixtures (sinks, toilets, faucets)
    • Water tanks

15. Doors and Windows

  • Process: Installing frames and closures.
  • Materials:
    • Wooden or metal doors
    • Window frames
    • Glass panes
    • Hinges and locks

16. Painting

  • Process: Applying paint to surfaces for finish and protection.
  • Materials:
    • Primer
    • Paint (interior and exterior)
    • Putty
    • Brushes and rollers
    • Whitewash
    • Masking tape

17. Final Touches

  • Process: Completing small details for aesthetics and functionality.
  • Materials:
    • Sealants
    • Caulking
    • Decorative items
